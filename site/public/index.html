What you can do

Three collections. One way to read.

Follow-along recitation and voice-or-text search work across all three — each with its own way in.

The Qur'an

114 surahs · 6,236 āyāt
  • Recite and it follows you word by word on the tajwīd mushaf — colored letters, with a natural paper-curl page turn.
  • Recite or type any āyah to jump straight to it, anywhere across the 114 surahs.
  • A gentle tajwīd-mistake review after you recite, so you notice the slips.

Adhkār & Duʿās

Hisn al-Muslim · ~260
  • Browse by occasion — morning, evening, sleep, travel, and the rest of Hisn al-Muslim.
  • Recite a duʿā and it follows along word by word, just like the mushaf.
  • Recite or type a fragment to find the one you mean.

Hadith

Bukhari + Muslim · 12,000+
  • Recite a fragment — or just type a word you remember — to find the hadith.
  • Open it and it follows your recitation word by word too.
  • Matches come back with your words highlighted, so you spot the right one fast.

On-device & private

Recitation is recognized entirely on your phone. No account, no cloud — your audio never leaves the device.

Voice or text

Recite it, or only remember a word and type it. Every collection searches both ways and highlights what you said.

How it works

Open it, recite, and it keeps up

01

Open or tap the mic

Open a surah, du'ā, or hadith — or just tap the mic to start from anywhere.

02

Recite aloud

Whether it's a surah, a duʿā, or a hadith, Sanad listens on-device and follows every word, highlighting your place as you go.

03

Find or move on

Lost your place, or want another? Recite or type a fragment — it finds and opens it.

Your recitation, your device

Private by design

  • ✓On-device recitation — speech is processed on your phone, never on a server.
  • ✓Your audio never leaves your phone — nothing is uploaded or recorded to the cloud.
  • ✓No account required — open the app and recite; there is nothing to sign up for.
  • ✓Opt-in, anonymous analytics only — off by default, never tied to you or your voice.
  • ✓No ads, no selling data — the app is not in the business of your attention.
